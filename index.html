ABOUT CARIT
The Center for Applied Research in Information Technology (CARIT) at Kennesaw State University is a research center that provides high quality survey research to students and other applicants. The goal of CARIT is to continously implement new ways to solve problems in today's society. The CARIT program is in need of student assistants in both the undergraduate and graduate level for upcoming projects and contracts.
